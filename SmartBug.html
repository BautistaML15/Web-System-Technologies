The Purpose of Optimizing Your Homepage

Overall, the purpose of your website is to market your products or services and turn website visitors into leads and, eventually, customers—and your homepage is no different.

Build trust and credibility with your visitors by linking to popular content on your homepage.

Pick two to four colors for your website and marketing materials and not deviate from those.

A website that is designed to adapt to whatever device a visitor is using allows for easy reading and scrolling without constantly adjusting your screen.
